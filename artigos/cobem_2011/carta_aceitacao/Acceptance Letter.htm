*DBC (Digital Board Communications) are an upgrade of Ordinary Posters to a kind of Digital Posters. However it is NOT a simple static Digital Poster. It goes beyond by allowing the speaker to show around 4 to 5 dynamic slides or even a small video. By using the provided LCD/Plasma or LED TVs DBC presentations are stylish to look at and the screens are designed to display high quality content that can be scheduled or looped.
